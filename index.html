About

Born and raised in New Mexico, Kobe Holmes grew interested in the rich sound of the Albuquerque area. Inspired by the unique sounds that came from the local area and the introduction to music production from close friends, Kobe slowly became more and more into the practice. As Kobe grew up, Hip-Hop music production had always caught his eye and influenced his decision to move out to New Orleans to develop his skills and grow his network. When working with such artists Kobe strives to create a unique and authentic sound for each of the artists he works with.
